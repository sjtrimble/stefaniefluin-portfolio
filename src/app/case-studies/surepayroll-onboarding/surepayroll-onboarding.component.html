A CASE STUDY

SurePayroll Onboarding

Redesigning the online customer enrollment experience.

Client

SurePayroll

SurePayroll is a Paychex Company focused on providing small business owners with easy-to-use online payroll services.

Role

UX Lead and Engineer

user flows, wireframing, visual design, prototyping, design system design, front-end development

Project Summary

The goal of this project was to create a new and improved streamlined onboarding experience for SurePayroll's small business customers. The primary focus was to increase the conversion rate and update the UI and aesthetics to a more modern look and feel.

The Challenge

The primary challenge of this project was that payroll accounting is a very complex system with lots of rules, regulations, and exceptions. Providing a seamless experience to the user while navigating the payroll intricacies smoothly was something we aimed to provide in the design and experience.

There were branding and design constraints to consider as SurePayroll wanted to refresh their outdated experience while remaining similar enough to their branding guidelines at the time.

The Solution

Summary
  • Immersive On-Site Workshops: Held several sessions with management, stakeholders and subject matter experts to understand the existing onboarding experience, goals, desires, and complexities of payroll accounting.
  • Ideate.
  • Design, implement, iterate.
Low Fidelity Wireframes

The project kicked off with a week-long discovery phase that included technical, subject matter expert and executive leadership interviews and working sessions. Once the goals and payroll accounting caviats were established, I produced low fidelity wireframes and a clickable prototype that demonstrated the user flow of the new onboarding experience.

Design System

SurePayroll was presented with various visual explorations to help guide their vision on their branding refresh for this particular application. They wanted to stay close to their current visual framework. The outcome was a design system that accounted for typography, colors, and all major components such as buttons, alerts, badges, and more.

The Results

  • Increased overall conversion rates by 24%
  • Increased conversion by 7.3% with complex payroll history customers
I'm grateful for this opportunity and having had the chance to have a material impact through the application of human centered design.
